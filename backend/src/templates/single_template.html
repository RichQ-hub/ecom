This pillar evaluates how a company impacts and manages the natural environment. It includes factors such as climate change policies, carbon footprint, and greenhouse gas emissions. Governance Metrics

Governance involves the internal system of practices, controls, and procedures a company adopts to govern itself, make effective decisions, comply with the law, and meet the needs of external stakeholders. This includes board diversity and structure, executive compensation, transparency, ethics, and shareholder rights. Strong governance ensures accountability and helps prevent corruption and conflicts of interest, fostering long-term sustainability and trust with investors and the public.
